Electric cars are more energy-efficient compared to ICE vehicles. While conventional gasoline engines waste a significant portion of the fuel's energy as heat, electric cars convert a higher percentage of the energy stored in their batteries into forward motion. This increased efficiency means electric vehicles can travel more miles on the same amount of energy, leading to cost savings and reduced reliance on fossil fuels.
